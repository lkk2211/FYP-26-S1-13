Property Guides & Tools

Everything you need to navigate Singapore's property market.

HDB Resale — Buying Process & Key Policies

1

Check Eligibility

SC/PR citizenship, age ≥21, valid family nucleus (married/parent/sibling scheme). Income ceiling applies for grants, not resale purchase.

2

HLE / Bank Approval

Get HDB Loan Eligibility (HLE) letter if taking HDB loan, or in-principle approval from bank. HLE valid 6 months.

3

Register Intent to Buy

Register on HDB Resale Portal. One active registration per household at a time.

4

Option to Purchase (OTP)

OTP fee: $1–$5K depending on flat price. Valid 21 calendar days. Exercise within the period to proceed.

5

Submit Resale Application

Buyer and seller both submit within 7 days of OTP exercise. HDB reviews in ~8 weeks.

6

Completion & Keys

Keys collected at HDB Hub. CPF/cash payment settled. BSD payable within 14 days of completion.

CPF Housing Grants (Resale)

  • Enhanced CPF Housing Grant (EHG): Up to $120K. Income ≤$9K/month. HDB loan only.
  • Family Grant: $50K (SC+SC) or $40K (SC+PR). Income ≤$14K.
  • Proximity Housing Grant (PHG): $30K (live with parents) or $20K (within 4km).
  • Singles Grant: $25K for SC singles aged ≥35 buying 2–4 room resale.
  • Grants credited to CPF OA, not cash. Must be first-time HDB buyers.

Minimum Occupation Period (MOP)

  • Standard MOP: 5 years from key collection (resale or BTO).
  • PLH flats (Prime Location): 10 year MOP, subsidy clawback applies.
  • After MOP: Can sell on open market, rent out entire flat, or buy private property.
  • During MOP: Cannot own private residential property. Can rent out spare rooms.

HDB Loan vs Bank Loan

  • HDB Loan: Rate = 0.1% above CPF OA (currently 2.6%). Up to 80% LTV. No cash down.
  • Bank Loan: Up to 75% LTV. Min 5% cash + 20% CPF/cash down payment.
  • MSR (HDB): Monthly repayment ≤30% of gross monthly income.
  • TDSR: All debt obligations ≤55% of gross income (applies to bank loans).

Lease Decay & CPF Limits

  • CPF usage: Pro-rated if flat lease <60 years at point of purchase.
  • Lease Consideration Policy: CPF withdrawal limit = price × remaining lease ÷ 99.
  • Bank loan restriction: Banks may decline loans if lease <30 years at end of loan tenure.
  • Valuation impact: Flats with <60yr remaining see accelerating price decline.

Private Condominium — Buying Process & Key Policies

1

Secure In-Principle Approval (IPA)

Get IPA from bank before making offers. TDSR ≤55% of gross income applies. Check your LTV (typically 75% first property).

2

Exercise OTP / S&P Agreement

Pay 1% option fee. OTP valid 14 days (resale). Exercise with 4–9% balance deposit. Sales & Purchase signed within 8 weeks.

3

Pay BSD & ABSD

BSD payable within 14 days of OTP exercise. ABSD (if applicable) also due within 14 days. Cannot use CPF for ABSD.

4

Legal Completion

Conveyancing via solicitor. Completion typically 10–12 weeks from S&P. Balance payment via CPF + cash + bank disbursement.

ABSD Rates (2023)

  • SC 1st property: 0%
  • SC 2nd property: 20%
  • SC 3rd+ property: 30%
  • PR 1st property: 5%
  • PR 2nd+ property: 30%
  • Foreigner: 60%
  • Entity: 65%
  • ABSD remission available for married SC/PR couples downgrading (conditions apply).

SSD & Seller Restrictions

  • SSD (Seller's Stamp Duty): If sold within 3 years of purchase.
  • Year 1: 12% · Year 2: 8% · Year 3: 4%
  • No SSD after 3 years from purchase date.
  • HDB owners: Must sell HDB within 6 months of private property completion.
  • Rental income: Taxable. Declare via IRAS annually.

TDSR & MSR Rules

  • TDSR ≤55%: All monthly debt repayments ÷ gross monthly income. Applies to all property loans.
  • MSR ≤30%: Mortgage repayment ÷ gross income. Applies to HDB loans and bank loans for HDB flats only.
  • Stress test: Banks compute TDSR at 4% interest floor (stress test rate).

CPF Usage Guide

  • • CPF OA can fund up to 100% of property price (subject to Valuation Limit and Withdrawal Limit)
  • • For HDB loan: no cash down payment required; up to 80% LTV
  • • For bank loan: min 5% cash + 20% CPF/cash down payment; up to 75% LTV
  • • CPF usage is pro-rated if remaining lease < 60 years at time of purchase
  • • CPF withdrawal limit = property value × remaining lease ÷ 99
  • • Rates are subject to change based on MAS and HDB policy
  • • For more information, please visit the HDB website
